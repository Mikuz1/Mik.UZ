About

Mik.UZ

DJ and producer from Ukraine. 15 years in trance and progressive house music.
Founder of MoreFun Records — Sub Label of Coffein Music Group. Key tracks:
A.L.Y.S., Mik.UZ - "Together" (Abora Recordings / Narratology Recordings)
Mik.UZ - "Mavka's Heart" (MoreFun Records)
Mik.UZ - "From Before" (Fuzion Records)
Mik.UZ - "Emotions" (West Drift Records)
Mik.UZ - "Two To One" (Tronic B7 Records)
Mik.UZ - "Trust" (Dirt, Lies & Audio Recordings)
Mik.UZ - "Split" (MoreFun Records)
Mik.UZ - "Energetic Girl (Streyk Remix)" (MoreFun Records)
Co-producer of Loud Dynamics — electronic trance house collective with official Beatport profile and charting releases.
Hosted KINGS OF TRANCE on international radio Trance Energy Radio. Weekly live broadcasts featuring DJ sets, promoting own releases and international artists.
Resident at clubs Picasso (2009–2020), Split Club, ZUZU Club. Festival performances in Lviv (Aquarium Shklo, Olesky Castle).
